Vegan brownies

  • Time: 35 minutes
  • Serves: 12
Ingredients Quantity
bitter cocoa powder 3/4 cup
whole wheat 2 cups
sugar 2 cups
baking powder 2 teaspoons
salt 2 teaspoons
oil 3/4 cup
soy milk 2 cups

Steps:

  1. Turn the oven on to 175°C (bottom heat)

  2. Mix the flour, dark cocoa powder, baking powder and salt. Then include the sugar.

  3. Add the oil, vanilla essence, soy milk and with the help of your Bosch MFQ4780 stick mixer mix until you have a lump-free mass. Place a mold lined with parchment paper to bake (to prevent it from sticking when removing it from the oven).

  4. You can sprinkle the surface with chopped nuts and/or chocolate chips, it is also possible to add them to the inside of the brownie, when you finish beating the dough.

  5. Finally bake for 30/35 minutes at 175°C. Let cool 15 minutes before unmolding.
